SMAP

About SMAP:

SMAP is a program that calculates a mathematical score of all 50 US states based on the score weight the user sets up in the sliders. Different statistics can be selected by clicking on the options below the slider, after which a new slider will appear.

About Our Statistics:

SMAP uses a variety of statistics, each from varying locations. SMAP used Statista to collect the data. However, much of the data is public through US government agencies. Click on the small  ⓘ  next to each statistic to see more information.
